Why Kongfu...

- Studying Kung Fu has numerous benefits for individuals of all ages. Some are mental or spiritual while some are physical. Many kung Fu classes are aimed at improving the person as a whole instead of just focusing on enhancing the aspect of physical health. There are countless benefits of Kung Fu and are as follows.

- Shedding some few pounds is an aspiration to many people. For that reason, participating in Kung Fu will help burn calories as it increases movement, making it possible for individuals to shed body fat and excess weight.

- Great focus is necessary in the mastering or practicing of Kung Fu. Individuals learning Kung Fu from qualified experts will learn how to perform exercises designed not only to improve their bodies but their minds at the same time.

- Kung Fu classes are more than just physical exercises. The tradition and history of this art, developed originally by monks, will greatly improve the students’ spiritual side.

- Improvements in physical and mental health go beyond just enhancing an individual’s appearance and attitude. Addition of regular exercises help build the immune system and lowers the chances of health problems such as diabetes and heart disease.

- Training in Kung Fu can help improve self-esteem. Participants attain a sense of confidence though improving mental and physical skills taught by their instructors. As a result, an improved physical conditioning boosts their self-esteem. Since most Kung Fu classes teach students how to handle hostile situations, participants are gain confidence and self-esteem.

- The main disadvantage of taking Kung Fu classes is that you have to train vigorously on a regular basis, which may not be possible if you usually work tirelessly around the clock. On the other hand, Kung Fu classes are more costly as compared to aerobics.
